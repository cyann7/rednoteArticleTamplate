# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Rednote Markdown Studio is for content creators, operators, and independent publishers who want to turn Markdown drafts into Rednote-ready image posts without opening a heavy design tool. They are usually editing copy, checking visual rhythm, and exporting shareable images in one focused session.

## Product Purpose

The product lets people write structured Markdown, preview it through Rednote-style templates, adjust presentation details, and export static images for publishing. Success means a first-time user can understand the editor quickly, produce a polished post, and trust the exported result to match the preview.

## Brand Personality

Lightweight, clear, refined. The interface should feel approachable and fast, with enough craft to make exported content feel intentional.

## Anti-references

Avoid complex design-software chrome, dense admin dashboards, flashy marketing-site treatment, and overly decorative template-gallery behavior. The app should not feel like a heavy professional suite or a novelty generator.

## Design Principles

- Keep creation in flow: editing, previewing, adjusting, and exporting should stay close together.
- Make template differences visible without making the app shell noisy.
- Prefer familiar controls for repeated work, especially text formatting and export settings.
- Let the content carry the personality; the tool chrome should stay quiet.
- Treat preview accuracy as a product promise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for the app interface. Core actions should be keyboard reachable, icon-only controls need accessible names, focus states must be visible, and motion should respect reduced-motion preferences.
